Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Khoya

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and baking soda with milk to make smooth, crack-free balls.

  2. Deep fry the balls on low-medium heat until golden brown.

  3. Immerse fried balls in warm sugar syrup with cardamom and soak for 30 minutes.
